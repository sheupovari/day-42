Spicy Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, then cook until browned.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic to the pan, cook until softened.
